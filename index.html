About Me

Hello, my name is Gustav Heese! I am a passionate and dedicated student biology teacher at Kruinpark. My love for understanding the intricacies of life has led me to pursue a career in education, where I can share my knowledge and inspire the next generation of biologists.

I find the process of building web applications both challenging and rewarding, and I'm excited to continue expanding my skills in the world of programming.

Feel free to explore my portfolio to check out some of the coding projects I've worked on. If you'd like to get in touch or learn more about my background, please don't hesitate to reach out via the contact page. I'm always happy to connect with others who share my interests!
